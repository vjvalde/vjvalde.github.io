About Pamplona

Our Town's Story

Pamplona is a 5th class municipality in Negros Oriental, Philippines. Established in 1947, the town has grown into a vibrant community that honors its heritage while embracing progress. Known for agricultural production and warm Filipino hospitality, Pamplona offers a peaceful and authentic provincial experience.

Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and self-reliant municipality with empowered communities, sustainable livelihood, and quality social services that promote the general welfare of every Pamplonganon.

Mission

To provide efficient and effective delivery of basic services through good governance, transparency, and accountability. To promote sustainable development, preserve cultural heritage, and ensure the well-being of all residents.
